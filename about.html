about the owner

Build with Confidence, Build with Us

The founder began his professional journey as an Associate with Lokhandwala Builders, where he contributed to landmark developments such as Minerva and several other prestigious projects in Worli.

He later went on to co-found Alamdar Infrastructure, where the company undertook the significant responsibility of tenant management for the Bhendi Bazaar Redevelopment Project, led by the Saifee Burhani Upliftment Trust (SBUT). This project is widely recognized as India’s and Mumbai’s first large-scale cluster redevelopment initiative. During this tenure, he also successfully delivered a redevelopment project in Marine Lines, known as “Marine Palace” and “Raj Heights” in Sion.

In 2016, he embarked on a new entrepreneurial journey by establishing Zeenat Constructions, a civil contracting firm that has since delivered numerous projects across Mumbai for reputed developers.

Under his leadership, Zeenat Constructions also successfully developed a prominent commercial project in Surat — “OZ House,” strategically located in the educational and cultural hub of the city.
