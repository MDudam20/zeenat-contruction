Sanghvi Parsssva Eternity

Sanghvi Parsssva Eternity is a modern residential development located in Khetwadi, Grant Road, one of the well-established and centrally located areas of Mumbai. The project is being developed by Sanghvi Parsssva Enterprises LLP, known for delivering quality residential developments with a focus on durability and modern design.

The project covers a total construction area of approximately 53,641 square feet and consists of a 19-floor residential tower designed to provide comfortable and well-planned living spaces for urban residents.

Project Description

The construction of Sanghvi Parsssva Eternity began on 1st December 2024 and is currently under development. The project is planned with modern architectural concepts and strong construction standards to ensure safety, durability, and long-term quality.

Located in Khetwadi, Grant Road, the development enjoys excellent connectivity to major commercial areas, transport facilities, and essential city infrastructure. The project is designed to provide well-structured residential units with efficient space planning and modern building infrastructure.

Once completed, Sanghvi Parsssva Eternity will offer residents a well-developed residential environment in one of Mumbai’s prime city locations.

  • Contemporary residential tower design
  • High-quality construction materials
  • Efficient space planning
  • Prime location in Grant Road
  • Strong structural framework
  • Developed by an experienced real estate developery
  • Designed for modern city living
